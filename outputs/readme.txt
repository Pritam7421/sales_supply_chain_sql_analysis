📊 Output Snapshots

This folder includes the result files generated from SQL-based analysis performed on the project dataset.

Each section highlights a different area of business analysis:

01_Data_Overview → Understanding the dataset structure, markets, regions, and segments
02_Sales_Analysis → Analysis of monthly and yearly sales performance
03_Financial_Analysis → Revenue trends, profit insights, and P&L reporting
04_Customer_Market → Identification of top customers and high-performing markets
05_Product_Analysis → Evaluation of best-selling and top-performing products by division
06_Supply_Chain → Forecast accuracy, inventory planning, and demand-related insights

These outputs were created to support reporting, presentation, and business decision-making.

Note: The original dataset is not included due to data-sharing restrictions. Only the query results and analysis outputs are provided.
